PROFESSIONALS IN FOOTBALL TRAINING

Yadah FC was established by Prophet Walter Magaya in 2013. The club was mainly composed of church ushers and congregants during it's formative years. In 2015 Yadah FC gained entry into the ZIFA Division one in Eastern region. The miracle boys as they are affectionately known by their legions of fans won the ZIFA Division one Eastern region title in their maiden appearance. In 2017 Yadah FC got it's promotion into the top flight league and had never been chopped from the top flight league since then.

Prophet Walter Magaya is a very passionate man who doubles both as the owner and techincal advisor of the team.

The club mainly uses the national Sports Stadium for the it's home matches and the prospect training ground situated adjacent to the Yadah magnificent hotel as the training ground. The Yadah FC players traditionally don the red and white striped jerseys during home matches. Yadah FC is the first church owned club to gain entry into the top flight league.

For some 8 years the Club has flourished from small beginnings to now having a track with an excellent surface complimented by trackside facilities that are better than most and as good as the best. The season runs from March to September with meetings on the last Sunday of the month. Additionally there are some two day meets and extra events.
